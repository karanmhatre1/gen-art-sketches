"Character Stretch"

Monday, July 19, 2021
Javascript, Paper.js, Figma, After Effects

Exploration of the letter 'A' through manipulation of its height, width, and crossbar position. Use numbers 1-8 on your keyboard to control various characteristics of the letter 'A'.

Press [1-8] keys to explore variations. Alteratively, use the controls on the top-right of the screen.

Made a version that works with the leapmotion. And hooked up some synth sounds for added effects.
